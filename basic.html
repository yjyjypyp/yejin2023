We have developed our creativity and potential in schools and societies. Going back and forth between the two worlds of school and society, these two worlds have fostered our potential. Then, where are we now ahead of graduation? We thought about the position of our potential. We believe that CSM is where we develop our ability to float on our own to unleash our creativity. Thus, we set a keyword as 'Ready to rise' that represents students who are prepared to unleash their abilities. We expressed the position of potential as ourselves ready to emerge somewhere in both school and society.
