+ READ PROJECT DESCRIPTION
In schools and societies, students have developed their creativity and potential. The show identity design highlights the position of potential as students ready to emerge somewhere in both school and society.

We believe that the Central Saint Martins is the place where the students develop their ability to ‘float’ on our own to unleash their creativity. Each design keywords, including floating spheres, diverse patterns, and glass structures, symbolizes the student’s potential, the transformative impact of education, and CSM’s role in nurturing students to ascend to their potential as a educational and social space.
